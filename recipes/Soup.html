Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Whether you're feeling under the weather or your're just in need of a little comfort,there's nothing like homemade chicken noodle soup to warm you up from the inside out. With this shortcut recipe,you can have the best chicken noodle soup of your life on the table in just 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon buter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (1.4 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth,vegatable broth,chicken,egg, noodles,carrots,basil,oregano,salt,and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
